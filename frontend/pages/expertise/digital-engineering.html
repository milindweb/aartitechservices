IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Understanding what's included and what's quoted separately.

DOMAIN REGISTRATION

Custom domain registration (.com, .in, .org and other extensions) is available. Domain registration and annual renewal charges are quoted separately and remain under the client's ownership.

HOSTING & CLOUD INFRASTRUCTURE

Hosting or cloud infrastructure is not included in the development cost. The appropriate hosting solution is recommended based on your project requirements, ranging from shared cloud hosting to dedicated VPS or enterprise cloud infrastructure.

MAINTENANCE & TECHNICAL SUPPORT

Development charges cover design, development, testing, and deployment. Ongoing maintenance, security updates, backups, technical support, content updates, and future enhancements are available under separate Annual Maintenance Plans (AMC) or Monthly Support Plans.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The following services are quoted separately whenever applicable:

  • Domain Registration & Renewal
  • Web Hosting / Cloud Hosting / VPS
  • Business Email Services
  • SMS & WhatsApp APIs
  • Payment Gateway Setup & Transaction Charges
  • Google Workspace / Microsoft 365
  • Premium Plugins, Themes & Software Licenses
  • Third-Party API & Subscription Services
